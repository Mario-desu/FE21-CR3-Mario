About us

Opened in 1989, the Kobayashi was one of the first authentic Japanese Restaurants in Austria. Our founder Takayuki Kobayashi was born in Osaka, Japan. Since the start of his career as an apprentice in a traditional Japanese Izakaya in downtown Osaka, he has always been dreaming of opening his own restaurant. During his time in France in 1988 he met his current wife, who is Austrian. He decided to follow her to Austria, where he made his dream come true and opened the Restaurant Kobayashi in the center of Vienna. Despite the long history we are still one of the most well known Japanese Restaurants in the Austrian Capital
